# Obsidian Reporting

## Purpose

Obsidian reports convert quantitative outputs into qualitative research memory.

## Templates

- `obsidian-vault/Data/Data-Quality-Report-Template.md`
- `obsidian-vault/Market-Research/Market-Metrics-Report-Template.md`
- `obsidian-vault/Research/Inefficiencies/Inefficiency-Scan-Report-Template.md`
- `obsidian-vault/Backtests/Backtest-Result-Report-Template.md`
- `obsidian-vault/Strategies/Strategy-Research-Note-Template.md`

## Report Sections

- Title.
- Date.
- Market id.
- Period.
- Summary.
- Main metrics.
- Observations.
- Hypotheses.
- Limits.
- Next actions.
- Internal links.

## Principle

Reports are interpretations. The raw quantitative source remains Postgres.
