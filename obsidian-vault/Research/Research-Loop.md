# Research Loop

## Intake
Raw sources arrive in `resources`.

## Distillation
Scripts or n8n workflows convert raw sources into clean notes under `Sources`, `Ideas`, `Market-Research`, or `Tools`.

## Hypothesis
Actionable claims become strategy hypotheses linked to sources.

## Test
Hypotheses move to [[Backtests]] with reproducible assumptions, fixtures, and metrics.

## Decision
Each idea becomes integrated, rejected, or deferred with evidence.

## Review
Live incidents and failed hypotheses become [[Post-Mortems]].
