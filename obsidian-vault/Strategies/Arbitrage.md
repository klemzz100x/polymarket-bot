# Arbitrage

Arbitrage notes capture market inefficiencies that can be stated as testable equations after fees, slippage, latency, and inventory risk.

## Required tests
- Fee-adjusted edge.
- Latency sensitivity.
- Fill probability.
- Failure mode when one leg does not fill.
- Capital lockup.
